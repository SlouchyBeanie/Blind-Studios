Who We Are

We are two mid-twenty year old Iowa guys who provide a premier lighting experience! We love music, and we know our lights complete the full package entertainment that people crave. We have tons of experience in all genres of music ranging from full music festivals, to EDM DJs, Rock n' Roll bands, and slow blues artists. We come in, set-up, rock on, and tear down all in the same day! We travel all over the United States to do gigs, so hit us up and let's create that music scene that people want.

What We Offer

We offer full-fledge lighting. We can do a full loadout for most venue sizes. We have tons of experience in both small and large areas. Have an idea of what you're looking for? We can implement it. Just want a great light show to go with your music? No problem, we'll come in and do it all.
